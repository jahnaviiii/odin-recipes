Cheesy Sandwich

Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and processed Cheddar for a classic hot sandwich.

Ingredients

  • white bread (salted)
  • Block of processed cheese, grated (cheese slices will do)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 green chillies, chopped
  • 1 capsicum, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 3 potatoes, boiled and smashed
  • Coriander leaves, chopped
  • 2 tsp pepper powder
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes(optional)



Directions

  1. Mix all the veggies along with the seasoning

  2. Butter the bread on both sides

  3. Coat the buttered bread with the aloo mixture on one side

  4. Put some cheese, close it with another bread piece

  5. Grill or cook until cheese melts, and voila!


    6. Serve the sandwich with some nice ketchup and chips!!
